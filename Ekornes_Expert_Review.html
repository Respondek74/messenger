Recommendations for a
seamless purchase experience
The brand emotionality is strong –
the purchase process must
live up to this standard.
1
UX · Configurator
Fundamentally redesign the configurator
Clear, unambiguously clickable elements with hover states. Active state always visible. Save configuration (account or link), load, share – not just share. Progress indicator for the user.
↑ Conversion · ↓ Drop-off rate
2
Omnichannel · Advisory · Dealer
Personal Contact & Preferred Dealer
A live chat or personal advisor accompanies the purchase process from the start – for complex questions about materials, fit, or delivery times. The saved preferred dealer can be brought in on request: they see the configuration and take over the consultation personally. This creates an early human connection – online and in-store.
↑ Close rate · ↑ Dealer relationship · ↓ Drop-offs from unanswered questions
3
Technology · Omnichannel
Seamlessly connect AR app & desktop
The AR app exists – but desktop configurations are not visible within it. This system disconnect must be resolved: recliners created in the online configurator must be directly accessible in the app to place them in your own room. A shared account as common data layer connects both channels.
↑ AR usage · ↑ Purchase readiness · System disconnect resolved
4
UX · Decision Support
Compare configured products
Compare two individually configured models side by side – including different colour combinations, materials, comfort levels, and prices. Especially valuable when customers are weighing two options. Reduces cognitive load and accelerates the decision.
↑ Decision quality · ↓ Purchase drop-offs
5
Availability · Dealer Handover
Seamless configuration handover
The saved configuration loads directly at the preferred dealer – no restart, no explaining from scratch. Appointment booking directly from the shop. Position dealer channel and online channel as complementary: “Configure online, experience in-store.”
↑ Qualified leads · ↑ Customer satisfaction · Dealer workload reduced
6
Personalisation · CRM
Personalised shop entry
See your own context immediately on login: saved configurations, recommendations based on purchase history, complementary products for your existing recliner (e.g. matching ottomans, tables), latest arrivals, and care products. The shop welcomes you – not just lists.
↑ Return rate · ↑ Cross-sell · ↑ Customer retention
