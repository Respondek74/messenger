# Ekornes / Stressless® — Customer Journey Narrative

*Seven phases of a seamless experience — from digital inspiration through the haptic bridge to in-store purchase and beyond.*

---

## Phase 1 — Discover
**First Contact**

**Emotion:** Curiosity (60%)

**Touchpoints:** Social Media Ad · Word of Mouth · Google Search · Magazine / PR

**Experience:**
A lifestyle video shows a Stressless® recliner in the context of a beautifully designed Scandinavian home. The viewer senses immediately: this isn't about a piece of furniture — it's about a way of living.

**Customer Actions:**

- Click through to website or social profile
- First brand encounter with Stressless®

**Required Systems:** CMS · Social Media · SEO / Analytics

---

## Phase 2 — Explore
**Interactive House**

**Emotion:** Inspiration (75%)

**Touchpoints:** stressless.com · Interactive House Experience

**Experience:**
Instead of a conventional category navigation, the visitor enters a virtual house. They move through rooms — living room, home office, dining room — discovering products in context. Each room tells a story. A short Comfort Profile quiz personalises the experience from the start.

**Customer Actions:**

- Complete Comfort Profile (seating habits, style, room size)
- Explore rooms, discover products in real-life contexts
- Receive personalised product recommendations

**Required Systems:** CMS / Headless Frontend · AI Personalisation Engine · Customer Data Platform

---

## Phase 3 — Configure
**Heart of the Experience**

**Emotion:** Excitement (92%)

**Touchpoints:** Product Configurator · WebAR

**Experience:**
The configurator becomes the centrepiece — much like automotive manufacturers have perfected. Photorealistic real-time 3D rendering: swap leathers, change wood finishes, adjust sizes — every change visible instantly. Project the chair into your own living room via WebAR. Save, share, and refine configurations together with your partner.

**Customer Actions:**

- Configure product individually (size, leather, wood, base)
- WebAR: experience furniture in your own room
- Share configuration with partner (Shared Wishlist)
- Build a shortlist of favourites

**Required Systems:** 3D Configurator Engine · WebAR · PIM / Product Data · Master Data Hub

---

## Phase 4 — Touch
**Haptic Bridge**

**Emotion:** Anticipation (85%)

**Touchpoints:** Configurator Upsell · Micro-Commerce · Email Nurturing

**Experience:**
Digital meets physical — but smarter than a free sample. From the configurator, the customer orders a small everyday product made from their chosen leather: a set of coasters, a leather bookmark, or a key fob. For under €10, they live with their material before committing to the chair. Every time they place a glass on the coaster or reach for their keys, they touch and feel the quality of their future Stressless®. This turns passive sampling into emotional investment.

**Customer Actions:**

- Choose a haptic product in the configurator (coasters, bookmark, key fob, cable pouch, …)
- Product is made from the exact leather of their configuration
- Live with the material in daily life — feel the quality firsthand
- Receive saved configuration + dealer recommendation by email

**Required Systems:** Micro-Commerce Checkout · PIM / Product Data · Logistics · Marketing Automation (Marketo) · CRM

---

## Phase 5 — Experience
**Dealer Visit**

**Emotion:** Trust (88%)

**Touchpoints:** Dealer Locator · Appointment Booking · Showroom

**Experience:**
The customer books an in-store consultation with the nearest dealer. The dealer sees the Comfort Profile, saved configurations, and the haptic product they ordered — they already know which leather the customer has been living with. The conversation doesn't start from zero — it starts where the customer left off online. A warm handover from digital to physical.

**Customer Actions:**

- Find a dealer nearby
- Book appointment with configuration as briefing
- Bring your haptic product — the dealer knows your material
- Receive personalised advice based on the digital profile

**Required Systems:** CRM / Dealer Portal · Appointment Booking System · Dealer Enablement Dashboard

---

## Phase 6 — Purchase
**Seamless Checkout**

**Emotion:** Satisfaction (80%)

**Touchpoints:** Point of Sale (Dealer) · Online Checkout (D2C)

**Experience:**
Whether at the dealer or in a future online shop: the configuration is already stored, pricing is accurate across regions, delivery time is transparent. No re-entering data, no friction between digital and physical channels.

**Customer Actions:**

- Purchase at dealer or online
- Automated price calculation (regionally accurate)
- Delivery status tracking

**Required Systems:** Commerce Platform · ERP / SAP · Order Management · Automated Price Lists

---

## Phase 7 — Belong
**Post-Purchase**

**Emotion:** Belonging (70%)

**Touchpoints:** Email · App · Community

**Experience:**
After the purchase, the relationship truly begins: care instructions at the right moment, matching accessory recommendations, an invitation to the Stressless® community. Satisfied owners become brand ambassadors — and return for dining chairs, office chairs, or the next sofa.

**Customer Actions:**

- Receive care tips and onboarding content
- Discover accessories and complementary products
- Share experiences, join the community
- Cross-selling: explore additional product lines

**Required Systems:** Marketing Automation (Marketo) · CRM · Community Platform · AI Recommendations
