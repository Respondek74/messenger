# TTS Prompt — Stressless® Customer Journey Narrative

## Voice & Style Instructions

> **Voice:** Warm, confident, unhurried. A thoughtful narrator — not a presenter. British English pronunciation. Think podcast storytelling, not keynote stage.
>
> **Pace:** Conversational and flowing. No announcements, no numbered lists. The listener should feel like someone is walking them through an idea over coffee. Natural breathing pauses between thoughts — no hard cuts.
>
> **Tone:** Quietly compelling. The kind of voice you keep listening to even when you're doing something else. Never dramatic, never selling. Just someone who clearly understands what they're talking about.
>
> **Pronunciation:** "Stressless" with soft emphasis on the first syllable. "Ekornes" as "EH-kor-ness". "WebAR" as "Web A R".
>
> **Pause notation:** *[pause]* marks a deliberate breath or beat — roughly 1.5 seconds. *[long pause]* marks a longer moment of silence — roughly 3 seconds. Use these to let key ideas land.

---

## Script

It starts with a feeling.

*[pause]*

A short video, maybe on Instagram, maybe someone shared it — a Stressless recliner in a beautifully designed Scandinavian living room. And something clicks. This isn't about furniture. *[pause]* It's about how you want to live.

*[long pause]*

So you follow the thread. You land on the website — but instead of the usual product grid, you step into a virtual house. You wander through rooms. Living room, home office, dining area. Every room tells a small story. And a quick comfort quiz begins to shape what you see — the experience starts to feel like yours.

*[pause]*

That's when the configurator pulls you in. And this is really the centrepiece. Think of how the car industry does it — real-time 3D rendering, photorealistic, responsive. You swap leathers, change the wood finish, try different sizes. Everything updates instantly. You can even project the chair into your own living room through WebAR. Save it, share it with your partner, come back to it later. *[pause]* This is where excitement peaks.

*[long pause]*

Now here's where it gets clever. Before committing to the chair, you order something small — a set of leather coasters, a bookmark, a key fob — made from exactly the leather you just configured. For under ten euros, you start living with the material. *[pause]* Every time you set down a glass or reach for your keys, you feel it. The texture, the quality. *[pause]* It's not a sample. It's the beginning of a relationship with the product. Passive interest becomes emotional investment.

*[long pause]*

And when you're ready, you book a visit at the nearest dealer. But the conversation doesn't start from scratch. The dealer already sees your comfort profile, your saved configurations, even the leather you've been living with. They know what matters to you. *[pause]* It's a warm handover — digital to physical — and that's where trust is built.

*[pause]*

The purchase itself is seamless. Whether it happens in-store or online, the configuration is already there. Pricing is transparent, delivery times are clear. No re-entering data, no friction between channels.

*[long pause]*

But the relationship doesn't end at the doorstep. *[pause]* That's actually where it begins. Care tips arrive at the right moment. Accessories that match your model. An invitation into the Stressless community. Satisfied owners become ambassadors — and eventually come back for a dining chair, an office chair, or the next sofa.

*[long pause]*

Seven moments, one continuous arc. From the first spark of curiosity *[pause]* to a lifelong connection with the brand.
